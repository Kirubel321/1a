American College of Technology (ACT) is an accredited higher learning institution established with a mission of producing internationally competent professionals at undergraduate and postgraduate level. The College is also providing short term computer Science and softs skill trainings including project management, communication skills, presentation skills, problem-solving skills, conflict resolution, leadership skills, emotional intelligence, time management, ethics, teamwork, adaptability and related.

The program is delivered in collaboration with the US based Maharishi University of Management (MUM) and local staffs with significant industry experience.
